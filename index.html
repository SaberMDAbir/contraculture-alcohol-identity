Support Your Child's Wellbeing

Parents don't want to see their children fall into alcohol. Invest in school-based programs.

  • Funnel money into public schools across the nation to teach alcohol education.
  • Start teaching from 8 years old when children are most impressionable.
  • Partner with other early education programs to facilitate learning growth.
    i.e.: Sex education, Substance Abuse Prevention

CC | Alcohol supports early education

on alcohol abuse and prevention in South Korea. Our services assist the government offices, hospitals, and schools to facilitate information to a wide population of drinkers in the country and especially to the next generation of potential drinkers, thus increasing the risk of alcohol prevention.

    Belief

  • South Korea has an excellent infrastructure set in place for information disseminate.

    • Proof Points:

  • Similar to Google, South Korea has NAVER Government information is often texted and emailed to residents

Challenge

  • South Korean citizens lack the basic information and education behind alcohol overdose and diseases stemming from alcohol abuse.

    • Proof Points:

  • Rate of high-risk drinking increases daily.
  • Indirect and direct alcohol marketing plays a huge role in alcohol sales in SK.

Model

  • CC | Alcohol provides services that funnel fundraising money to schools, social programs, and hospital programs. We are the safe-haven for all alcoholics.

    • Proof Points:

  • International support from NIAAA and NCAAD.
  • We will provide the foundation to support schools nationwide on early education and prevention.
